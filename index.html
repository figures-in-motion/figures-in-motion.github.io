Dear fans,

It's with great sorrow that we are ending Figures in Motion. :( For the last few months, we were not able to work together at the same pace and all keep up with our ambitions. We tried to save this project by looking for a new dynamic, to no avail.

We are deeply sorry.

Thanks a lot to all for your support and love! The fact that our music touched you in any way makes us so happy. We also met wonderful people along the way (cheers to the Fallenfest, Le Buzz, Jamendo…).

Take care and stay in the loop, more projects will certainly rise from this end!

What does that mean?


FAN You can share freely our music with everyone. Although if you want to support us you may direct people to this page to buy our music directly from us.

ARTIST You can use our music freely in your own creations. The only thing required is to credit us with a link to this page. If you want to do some RMXS, ask us the stems! And please drop a word if you use our songs in a video or some remixes. We strive to hear your awesome work!

PRO Are you looking for some new music to broadcast on your radio station, bar or shop? Go play our tracks! You just have to credit us to have a free, unlimited access to all our songs.
